Monkey D. Luffy

Also known as " Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat", is the main protagonist of the manga and anime. The founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Gol D. Roger. He believes that being the Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world.

Power/Ability

Devil fruit: Gum-Gum fruit (rubber human)

Roronoa Zoro

Also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates, Formerly a bounty hunter, the first member to join Luffy's crew. His dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world, in order to honor a promise he made to his deceased childhood friend

Power/Ability

Three sword style : a swordsmanship style which he created during his childhood training in Shimotsuki Village, Zoro is among the three most powerful combatants of the Straw Hats.

Nami

Also known as "Cat Burglar" Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates, Second member to join Luffy's crew. She was formerly a member of the Arlong Pirates and initially joined the Straw Hats so that she could rob them in order to buy back her village from Arlong Pirates. However, she legitimately joined the Straw Hats after they rebelled against and defeated Arlong Pirates. Her dream is to make a map of the entire world.

Power/Ability

Clima-Tact: Weapon that is capable of producing a bubble of air. Art of Weather: using the Clima-Tact to manipulate the weather of her surroundings with certain combinations of these bubbles and Nami's skills in meteorology.

Usopp

Also known as "God" Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates He is the third to join Luffy's crew. Usopp dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea just like his father and lives every day in pursuit of living up to this dream.

Power/Ability

Sniper Skills and Creative Wit

Sanji

Also known as "Black Leg" Sanji, born as Vinsmoke Sanji,is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the fourth to join the crew. Born as the third son and fourth child of the Vinsmoke Family (former prince of the Germa Kingdom), His dream is to find the rumored chef's paradise, All Blue, which is where East Blue, West Blue, North Blue, and South Blue meet, along with their wildlife. He is one of the top three fighters of the Straw Hats, alongside Luffy and Zoro.

Power/Ability

Black Leg Style: resembles a unique combination of Brazilian Capoeira, French Savate and Korean Taekwondo.

Tony Tony Chopper

Also known as "Cotton Candy Lover" Chopper, he is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. Fifth to join Luffy's crew. Chopper is a reindeer that ate the Hito Hito no Mi, a Devil Fruit that allows its user to transform into a human. He Was taught how to be a doctor by his two parental figures, Doctors Hiriluk and Kureha. His dream is to one day become a doctor capable of curing any disease and wants to travel all across the world specifically in the hopes of accomplishing this dream.

Power/Ability

Devil fruit: Human-Human Fruit (able to transform into different battle forms)

Nico Robin

Also known as "Devil Child" and the "Light of the Revolution", she is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. sixth the join Luffy's crew. Sole survivor of the destroyed West Blue island Ohara, she is currently one of only two people in the world known to have the ability to read and decipher Poneglyphs, a skill which is considered forbidden and threatening to the World Government. Her dream is to find the Rio Poneglyph which tells the true history of the world.

Power/Ability

Devil fruit: Hana-Hana Fruit (power to reproduce her body parts (or her entire body) on any surface)

Franky

"Iron Man" Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates, seveth to join the crew. He is a cyborg due to an incident resulted in his body being heavily damaged. Franky's dream is to create a ship and go around the world, so he builted the Thousand Sunny and joined the Straw Hat Pirates to fulfill his dream.

Power/Ability

His body is equipped with a wide variety of weapons, including rockets, an extendable right arm, the ability to breathe powerful flames as well as being able to release powerful blasts of air from multiple parts of his body.

Brook

"Soul King" Brook is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates, one of their two swordsmen and eighth the join the crew. Originally a member of the Rumbar Pirates, he died and was resurrected through the power of the devil fruit he ate. However, due to the amount of time it took for his soul to find his body, it was reduced to a skeleton, keeping only his afro intact.

Power/Ability

Devil fruit: Revive-Revive Fruit (enhances the user's soul to the point where they resurrect after their first death, allowing them to live a second time and to use several other soul-based abilities)

Jinbe

"Knight of the Sea" Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and the newest member to join the crew. Jinbe is a whale shark fish-man and a powerful master of Fish-Man Karate. His dream is to fulfill his former captain Fisher Tiger's dying wish of coexistence and equality between humans and fish-men.

Power/Ability

Fish-Man Karate: Can use to knock away many opponents at once and grapple with and defeat powerful enemies several times his size.
